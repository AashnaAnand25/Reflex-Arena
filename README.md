# Reflex Arena ⚡

A brutal browser-based reflex game. Click when it goes green. Fake-outs will destroy you. Climb the leaderboard.

**[Play it live →](https://reflex-arena.vercel.app)**

---

## How it works

Hit the arena the instant it flashes green. The faster you click, the more points you earn. Build combos by hitting greens back-to-back — every streak multiplies your score. Fall for a fake-out or click too early and your combo resets.

## Game modes

| Mode | Rounds | Fake-out rate | Difficulty |
|------|--------|--------------|------------|
| Classic | 8 | 25% | Normal |
| Daily | 6 | 35% | Hard |
| Survival | 12 | 40% | Brutal |

## Features

- Three game modes with increasing difficulty
- Combo multiplier system — streaks boost your score bonus
- Live combo decay bar so you can see your window closing
- Per-mode leaderboard with persistent personal best
- Shareable emoji score card (Wordle-style) to challenge friends
- Full retro neon arcade aesthetic with scanline overlay

## Stack

Pure HTML + CSS + vanilla JavaScript. Zero dependencies. Works in any modern browser.
